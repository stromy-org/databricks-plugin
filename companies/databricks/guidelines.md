# Databricks — Brand Guidelines (recovered)

Source: public brand-intelligence harvest (databricks.com, press kit, live-site DOM inspection),
2026-07-06. This is a reconstruction from public evidence for internal showcase use — not an
authored Databricks document.

## Identity

Databricks is the Data and AI company. Founded 2013 by the original creators of Apache Spark,
Delta Lake and MLflow. Unified, open lakehouse platform for data engineering, data science,
machine learning and analytics.

## Visual grammar (recovered, live-site evidence)

- **Backgrounds:** Full-bleed flat-color section bands, alternating dark navy and light cream.
  No gradients, no background imagery.
- **Motifs:** Solid-color rectangular tiles (accent-orange, dark-navy) used as graphic content
  blocks — a distinctive substitute for photography in content grids.
- **Elevation:** Signature "floating card on dark band" — a soft, multi-layer diffuse shadow
  lifting light-colored cards off dark section backgrounds.
- **Iconography:** Two families — thin-stroke cool grey-blue line icons, and bolder solid-fill
  illustrative shapes in navy and accent-orange.
- **Motion:** Lottie-based animated icons on product/feature tiles (not static).
- **Patterns / Gradients:** Not used on the public site — kept flat and minimal by design.
